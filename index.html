about

Sierra Seetin is a filmmaker and creative producer based between New York City, Upstate New York, and Boston. She works as a producer, director, art department creative, and freelance production assistant on short narrative, experimental, body horror, indie, and feature projects, with experience at Ghetto Film School, E.S.O. Media, and Future Proof Films, and is a member of Women in Film & Video New England.
