about

I am Sierra, a filmmaker and business student trying to make my life look slightly more organized than my Google Drive. I study Film and New Media and Business at Wheaton College in Massachusetts and grew up in Queens, which means I walk fast, talk fast and accidentally treat every to-do list like a side quest. I like cameras, stories, tech experiments and any excuse to turn a random idea into something on a screen.

work

I direct, shoot and edit short films, documentary pieces and little internet projects that live somewhere between personal and professional. My favorite sets feel like organized chaos where everyone knows what they are doing but still laughs a lot.

  • short films · narrative and character driven pieces
  • documentary · real people, real places, good lighting
  • photography · behind the scenes, portraits, small details
