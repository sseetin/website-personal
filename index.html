About

I am Sierra, a Film and New Media and Business double major at Wheaton College in Massachusetts who somehow turned an obsession with movies, tech and spreadsheets into a personality. I grew up in Queens so I walk fast, talk fast and tend to collect projects the way other people collect keychains. I like telling stories on screen, building things in labs and making organized chaos look intentional.
